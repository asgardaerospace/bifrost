Version: 1.0

Organization: Asgard Aerospace

---

# 1. Purpose

The Execution Engine is the operational heartbeat of Bifrost.

Its purpose is to:
- coordinate execution across the organization
- transform intelligence into action
- orchestrate workflows
- route operational pressure
- prioritize execution
- maintain mission momentum

The Execution Engine exists to prevent:
- operational drift
- stalled workflows
- disconnected actions
- execution blindness
- fragmented coordination

Bifrost does NOT exist to:
- display information passively
- track tasks in isolation
- act as a static project management system

Bifrost exists to:
- accelerate execution
- sustain operational momentum
- coordinate organizational action
- maintain strategic alignment

---

# 2. Core Philosophy

## Execution Is The Center Of The OS

Everything inside Bifrost ultimately exists to:
- advance missions
- coordinate action
- remove blockers

- maintain operational velocity

Operators should feel:

# 4. Execution Queue System

## Purpose

The execution queue is:
- the operational heartbeat of the company
- the primary action surface of Bifrost

---

## Queue Contents

The queue may contain:
- approvals
- follow-ups
- supplier reviews
- investor actions
- mission escalations
- operational blockers
- autonomy proposals
- intelligence-driven actions

---

## Queue Ordering Philosophy

Queue priority is determined by:
- mission relevance
- operational pressure
- urgency
- strategic impact
- dependency propagation

NOT:
- creation timestamp alone

---

## Queue Categories

### Critical
Immediate operational threat or strategic importance.

### Active
Operationally important execution items.

### Ambient
Low-pressure operational suggestions.
