# Bifrost Intelligence System
## Strategic Signal and Operational Awareness Framework for Asgard Aerospace

Version: 1.0  
System: Bifrost OS  
Organization: Asgard Aerospace

---

# 1. Purpose

The Intelligence System transforms external and internal signals into operational awareness and strategic action.

This system exists to:
- monitor the aerospace and defense environment
- detect meaningful strategic changes
- compress signal overload into actionable intelligence
- route relevance into missions
- surface operational implications early
- increase organizational awareness velocity

The Intelligence System is NOT:
- a news feed
- a media dashboard
- a passive monitoring system

It is:
- a strategic signal processing layer
- a mission-aware awareness engine
- an operational relevance system
- an intelligence propagation network

---

# 2. Core Philosophy

## Intelligence Exists To Drive Action

Signals only matter if they:
- affect missions
- alter priorities
- create opportunities
- increase risks
- change operational posture

The system should continuously answer:

1. What changed?
2. Why does it matter?
3. Which missions are affected?
4. What should happen next?
5. What happens if ignored?

---

## Signal Compression Over Information Volume

The Intelligence System should:
- reduce noise
- eliminate low-value information overload
- compress complexity
- prioritize strategic relevance

The goal is:
- strategic clarity
- not informational completeness

---

## Intelligence Must Propagate

Signals should move through the organization dynamically.

Example:

```text
Defense funding increase
    ↓
Investor appetite increase
    ↓
Competitive acceleration
    ↓
Supplier demand increase
    ↓
Mission reprioritization
```

Bifrost must model this propagation.
