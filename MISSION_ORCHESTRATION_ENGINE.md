- aligns teams around strategic objectives
- routes operational effort

- orchestrates organizational movement

This document governs:
- mission coordination

- mission prioritization

The Mission Orchestration Engine exists to prevent:

Bifrost is NOT:

# 6. Mission Lifecycle System

## Purpose

Manage mission evolution continuously.

---

## Lifecycle States

### Proposed
Mission identified but inactive.

### Active
Execution underway.

### Focused
Pressure elevated and coordination intensified.

### Escalated
Strategic intervention required.

### Stabilized
Execution recovering.

### Completed
Objectives achieved.

### Archived
Mission preserved historically.
