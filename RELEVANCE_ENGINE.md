# Bifrost Relevance Engine
## Operational Prioritization and Attention Routing Framework for Asgard Aerospace

Version: 1.0  
System: Bifrost OS  
Organization: Asgard Aerospace

---

# 1. Purpose

The Relevance Engine is the cognitive prioritization system of Bifrost.

Its purpose is to determine:
- what matters
- when it matters
- who it matters to
- how strongly it should surface
- what actions should follow

The Relevance Engine exists to prevent:
- information overload
- operational blindness
- alert fatigue
- dashboard chaos
- fragmented prioritization

Without the Relevance Engine:
- every signal appears equal
- every alert competes for attention
- operators lose clarity
- the OS collapses into noise

The Relevance Engine transforms Bifrost into:
- a strategic compression system
- a mission-aware cognition layer
- an operational prioritization engine

---

# 2. Core Philosophy

## Relevance Over Completeness

Bifrost should NOT show:
- everything

Bifrost should show:
- the most operationally meaningful information
- at the correct moment
- in the correct context
- with the correct action path

---

## Attention Is a Strategic Resource

Operator attention is limited.

The system must:
- conserve attention
- route attention intelligently
- escalate only meaningful change
- suppress operational noise

---

## Operational Meaning Drives Visibility

Visibility should emerge from:
- mission impact
- operational pressure
- urgency
- strategic value
- dependency propagation

NOT:
- chronological order
- raw activity volume

Measure company-level significance.

---

## Inputs

- revenue implications
- manufacturing implications

- defense positioning
- operational continuity
- strategic differentiation

---

## Effects

## Purpose

---

## Inputs

- deadline proximity
- execution windows
- operational decay

- dependency timing

- mission coupling

## Effects
